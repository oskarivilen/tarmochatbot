Tasks of financial management

Our financial management responsibilities include the budget of the housing association, preparation of accounts and financial statements, consideration and rent control and payment transaction management. We also take care preparation of the housing company's tax return and contact with the tax authorities.

Obligations of the housing association

We prepare a housing company statutory rescue plan and energy certificate professionally in cooperation with the subscriber. We also make security plans with security professionals.
